= Code of Conduct
:icons: font

This project is committed to maintaining a respectful, professional, and
inclusive environment for all contributors and users.

== Our Pledge
We pledge to treat all participants with respect and to foster open,
constructive collaboration focused on the quality and integrity of the work.

== Expected Behavior
* Be respectful and considerate in language and tone
* Provide constructive, evidence-based feedback
* Assume good faith in discussions and reviews
* Focus on improving the content, not winning arguments

== Unacceptable Behavior
* Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
* Personal attacks or threats
* Publishing private or identifying information without consent
* Persistent disruptive or bad-faith behavior

== Enforcement
Project maintainers are responsible for interpreting and enforcing this
Code of Conduct.

Consequences for unacceptable behavior may include:
* Warning
* Temporary restriction from participation
* Permanent removal from the project

== Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a GitHub issue
labeled `conduct` or contact the maintainers through the organization
associated with this repository.
