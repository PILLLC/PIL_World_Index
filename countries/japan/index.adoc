== Overview
Japan is a constitutional monarchy with a parliamentary system of government.
It is the world’s fourth-largest economy by nominal GDP and a major global
industrial, technological, and financial power. Japan’s contemporary policy
environment is strongly shaped by demographic aging, fiscal sustainability
concerns, and regional security dynamics in East Asia. footnote:[Primary sources listed in the Sources Registry.]
