== Governance
Canada operates under a federal parliamentary system in which executive authority
is exercised by a prime minister and cabinet accountable to Parliament. Powers
are divided between federal and provincial governments, with provinces holding
significant responsibility for health, education, and social policy.

== Economy
Canada has a high-income, diversified economy that includes advanced services,
manufacturing, and significant resource-based sectors. Economic performance is
influenced by external trade, commodity markets, and productivity trends.

== Demographics
Population growth in Canada is driven primarily by immigration, with natural
increase contributing a smaller share. Demographic patterns vary across regions
and provinces.

== Data Notes
* National and subnational data are primarily produced by Statistics Canada.
* Provincial data are generally robust; territorial data may be less granular.
