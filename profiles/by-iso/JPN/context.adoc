== Governance
Japan is a constitutional monarchy with a parliamentary system. Executive
authority is exercised by a prime minister and cabinet responsible to the
National Diet. Administrative authority is relatively centralized.

== Economy
Japan has a high-income, diversified economy with strengths in manufacturing,
technology, services, and finance. Long-term trends are shaped by demographic
change and productivity dynamics.
